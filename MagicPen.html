It depends on how much you use it and how many times a day. One pen may last 3 weeks, 2 weeks, 1 week... The more you use it, the quicker you'll see results.

Can I use it on top of my nail colour?

Yes.

The Magic Pen has exclusive technology with a high absorption rate. Therefore, you can use it on top of your nail colour. However, it doesn't work on top of solid adhesives, such as the superglue of SNS and other kinds of artificial nails.

It won't damage your regular nail polish after you use it.

To maximise the results, apply on the bare nails without colour. For example, suppose your nails are damaged or peeling off, or you've recently removed artificial nails. In that case, using the product on your bare nails 3-4 times a day for a week is better. Later, you can apply it on top of your favourite colour once a day.

Can I use it on top of SNS?

Not on top, but you can still use it.

The main component of SNS is super glue (Cyanoacrylate). According to Nail Expert Alison Bowhill Hayes, "SNS is a super glue that's applied to nails and then dipped into a polymer (acrylic powder)" (source). No treatment can penetrate through such a strong adhesive.

However, you can still use the Magic Pen for cuticle hydration and in the gap between your cuticle and SNS. That will make your nails grow faster, though not with the same efficiency. If you try to use it on top of SNS, it won't penetrate.

Can I wash my hands?

Many customers have cleaner jobs or religious requirements to wash their hands frequently. All of them have noticed results without significantly affecting their routine.

The product is a NAIL CREAM, so it doesn't create a hard layer on top of your nails. Due to its high absorption rate, the Magic Pen penetrates deeply into the nail bed in minutes. Therefore, washing your hands many minutes after applying the product won't wear out. However, we recommend using the Magic Pen after you wash your hands, not immediately before, to ensure maximum effectiveness.
